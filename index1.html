About Us

Planning is an intellectual process which requires a manager to think before acting. It is thinking in advance. It is by planning the managers of organizations decide what is to be done, when it is to be done, how it is to be done and who is to do it.  Decision making is an integral part of planning. Planning is defined as process of choosing among alternatives. Obviously decision making will occur at many points in the planning process. In fact deciding which goals to pursue is probably the most important part of the planning process.  Managers must also decide which assumptions about the future and about the environment they will use in making their plans. In addition managers must decide how they will allocate their resources to attain their goals.
